nevmuse
=======

nevmuse is a distinct software component bundled with PythonMusic. It implements
the Zipf-based music-information-retrieval metrics used by PythonMusic's `zipf`
library (see src/zipf.py).

nevmuse was developed by the music / computer-science research lab of
Dr. Bill Manaris at the College of Charleston. Contributors named in the source
headers include:

   Bill Manaris, Tim Hirzel, Patrick Roos, John Emerson, Hector Mojica,
   Luca Pellicoro, Brian Muller, Brys Sepulveda, David Johnson, Brian Murtagh.
